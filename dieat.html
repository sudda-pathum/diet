Our Popular Diet Plans

Balanced Diet

Classic Balanced Diet

  • Proteins: Lean meats, fish, eggs, legumes
  • Carbs: Brown rice, whole grains, sweet potatoes
  • Veggies & Fruits: Variety, including leafy greens
  • Healthy fats: Nuts, olive oil, avocados

This plan provides all essential nutrients for energy and wellness, focusing on balance and moderation.

Mediterranean

Mediterranean Plan

  • Lots of vegetables and fruits
  • Whole grains and legumes
  • Olive oil as main fat source
  • Fish & moderate dairy
  • Fresh herbs and minimal processed foods

Inspired by traditional coastal diets for heart health and longevity.

Vegan

Plant-Based Vegan

  • All plant-based: vegetables, fruits, grains, legumes
  • Focus on whole foods
  • Nuts, seeds, and plant protein sources
  • B12 fortified foods included

For a compassionate, eco-friendly approach that supports overall health.

Low-Carb

Keto & Low-Carb

  • Non-starchy vegetables
  • High proteins and healthy fats
  • Minimal sugar and refined carb intake
  • Nuts, seeds, lean meats

Effective for weight management and stable energy levels.

Healthy Nutrition Tips

Stay hydrated: Aim for 6-8 glasses of water daily.
Fill half your plate with fruits and veggies in every meal.
Choose whole grains over refined grains for sustained energy.
Limit processed foods, added sugars, and salt intake.
Include healthy fats: nuts, seeds, olive oil, and avocados.
Control portions to avoid overeating; eat mindfully and slowly.
